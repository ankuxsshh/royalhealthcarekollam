Radhakrishnan Narayanan

Chairman and Managing Director

Indian Army - 15 years (1974-1989)

M.B.A with Marketing

Our esteemed Chairman Radhakrishnan.N served the country for 15 years. After emigrating to Oman in 1990 and starting a small construction business, due to hard work and the support of the local community and the government, he was able to build prestigious projects across the country including Shell and Omanoil fuel Stations, Schools, Universities, Safari Park Roads and plenty of other projects. He is currently the Chairman of The Royal Group which has interests in Construction, Petroleum, Healthcare, Retail and the Fitness industry.

Royal Healthcare is his dream project to give back and help develop the community he was born into by providing affordable healthcare and jobs in the community.

Director

Doctor of Medicine

Oman Medical College (Affiliated with West Virginia University, U.S.A.)

Diploma in Pharmacy – Fatima College of Pharmacy

Dr. Jayasree Radhakrishnan began her journey in healthcare with a Diploma in Pharmacy. After dedicating over 20 years to raising a family alongside Mr. Radhakrishnan, she was driven by a deep desire to contribute to the community in a more meaningful way. Today, she brings this knowledge and passion to Royal Healthcare, where she is committed to enhancing the quality of care and advancing community-focused medical services.
