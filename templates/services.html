SERVICES

We provide comprehensive, compassionate care with advanced medical treatments and skilled professionals.

ECG

ECG records the heart's electrical activity, helping detect irregularities and cardiac conditions effectively for accurate diagnosis and timely treatment.

CT-96 SLICE SIEMENS

High-speed CT scanner offering precise imaging for neurology, cardiology, and oncology with enhanced safety and faster diagnostics.

ULTRASOUND 4D

Provides real-time imaging for fetal health, organ functions, and diagnosis in cardiology, oncology, and prenatal care.

MODERN LAB

State-of-the-art laboratory facilities offering accurate diagnostics for blood analysis, pathology, and genetic screening.

DIGITAL X-RAYS

Digital X-rays provide efficient imaging for detecting fractures, infections, and tumors with reduced radiation exposure.


PALLIATIVE CARE

Provides relief from pain and symptoms, improving quality of life for patients with serious illnesses and offering compassionate support.

Emergency ambulance services ensuring swift patient transportation with medical facilities and trained paramedics.

MORTUARY

Specialized facility for preserving and handling deceased individuals with respect and proper documentation.

MOBILE MORTUARY

Portable facility providing hygienic storage of bodies during emergencies, ensuring dignity and sanitary conditions.
