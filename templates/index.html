ABOUT US

Royal Healthcare, located in Chathannoor, Kollam, Kerala, has been a trusted healthcare provider since 2005. Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional medical care and improve the health of our community. Over the years, we have earned a reputation for compassionate care, modern facilities, and a dedicated team committed to enhancing the well-being of those we serve. In 2016, we temporarily closed for a comprehensive renovation to upgrade our facilities and expand our services. However, the completion of our plans was delayed, especially due to the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. In response, we offered our facility to the government as a dedicated COVID-19 care center to support local efforts.

SERVICES

We provide comprehensive, compassionate care with advanced medical treatments and skilled professionals.

Medical Care

Medical care involves services provided by healthcare professionals to maintain or improve health, prevent illness, diagnose conditions, and treat diseases. It includes both physical and mental health support.

Palliative Care

Palliative care is specialized medical care aimed at providing relief from the symptoms and suffering of serious illnesses. It focuses on improving quality of life for patients and their families.

Emergency Care

Emergency care refers to the immediate medical attention provided to individuals experiencing acute health issues or injuries. It aims to stabilize, treat, and prevent further complications in urgent situations.

Fitness and Diet Based Services

Fitness and diet-based services offer personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and wellness coaching to help individuals achieve their health goals, improve fitness, and maintain a balanced lifestyle.

Mental and Physical Well-being counselling

Mental and physical well-being counseling focuses on supporting individuals in managing stress, emotional challenges, and physical health concerns, promoting holistic wellness through personalized strategies, guidance, and therapeutic techniques.
